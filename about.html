Sushant Kamble

Since I was young, I've loved tinkering with electronics, all thanks to my grandpa's guidance. I'd spend hours making my own gadgets, like power banks from old Macbook batteries or burglar alarms to safeguard my stuff from my brother's snooping hands.

When I got my hands on a computer, it opened up a whole new world of programming. I started coding and making apps in Visual Basics in my free time.

During summers, I'd dive into 3D modeling using Maya and turn simple Flash games into 3D adventures using Unreal Engine 4 by referring to YouTube tutorials.

These experiences ignited my passion for prototyping, that continues to drive me forward this journey.

Tooling and Workflow Optimization

AI-powered Web Scrapper


  • During my time at UT Austin, I developed a AI-powered tailored web scraping tool powered by Open AI APIs that created custom datasets and enabled two distinct research studies.

  • Study 1: The tool was instrumental in curating datasets comprising over a thousand IMDb directors, encompassing detailed information on their demographics, education, and career trajectories. These datasets were utilized to conduct socio-economic analyses.

  • Study 2: Additionally, the tool was employed to extract and analyze text from news articles sourced from the Global Database of Events, Language, and Tone (GDELT), facilitating analyses of internal crisis behaviors on a global scale.

Dynamic Survey with Advanced Branching


  • I undertook the task of facilitating user tests for researchers by developing a dynamic survey.

  • This survey required intricate logic to collect data and formulate questions tailored to each participant's specific criteria. To accomplish this, I was responsible for crafting custom JavaScript code for Qualtric's JS form engine, enabling sophisticated branching and question generation.

  • The extensive customization provided researchers with the capability to validate data in real-time, dynamically show/hide specific pages, and show only relevant questions as per participant's background.
